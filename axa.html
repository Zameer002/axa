About Us

Bharti AXA Life Insurance was formed and commenced business operations during the second wave of growth in the private Insurance space in 2006.

Bharti AXA brings together strong financial expertise of the Paris-headquartered AXA Group and Bharti Enterprises - one of India's leading business groups with interests in Telecom, agricultural business and financial services. Presently, the joint venture has a 51% stake from Bharti Group and 49% stake from AXA, in line with the regulatory framework on ownership in the Insurance sector.

Our company is the amalgamation of two great visionaries, Bharti Group and AXA, both come with diverse experiences and together bring a rich culture that forms the essence of Bharti AXA Life Insurance

Founded in 1976, by Sunil Bharti Mittal, Bharti has grown from being a manufacturer of bicycle parts to o of the largest and most respected business groups in India.
